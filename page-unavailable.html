A global media agency

The U.S. Agency for Global Media (USAGM) is the independent federal agency that oversees all U.S. civilian international media, including Voice of America, Radio Free Europe/Radio Liberty, the Office of Cuba Broadcasting, Radio Free Asia, and the Middle East Broadcasting Networks.

Mission

The mission of USAGM is to inform, engage, and connect people around the world in support of freedom and democracy.

For more information, please contact USAGM Public Affairs at (202) 203-4400 or by e-mail at pubaff@usagm.gov.
